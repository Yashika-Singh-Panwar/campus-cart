About us


Campus Cart is an online marketplace that caters to the needs of college students who want to sell their used items to other students on their campus. The website provides an easy and convenient platform for students to list and sell their items such as textbooks, electronics, furniture, clothing, and other items. The platform allows students to list their items for sale, along with pictures and a description of the product. Students can also set their own prices and negotiate with potential buyers by contacting them.

One of the key benefits of using Campus Cart is that it is exclusively available to college students, making it a safe and trustworthy environment to buy and sell items. Students can also easily browse through the website to find great deals on items they need, all without having to leave campus. In addition to providing a marketplace for buying and selling used items, Campus Cart also helps to reduce waste and promote sustainability by encouraging students to reuse and recycle items instead of throwing them away.
